Lunar Nodes & Precession

The Moon's orbit is tilted about 5.1° relative to Earth's orbit (the Ecliptic). The intersection points are the Lunar Nodes.

Ascending Node: Moon crosses North
Descending Node: Moon crosses South
Line of Nodes: Axis of intersection
