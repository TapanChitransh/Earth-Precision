Coordinate Systems & The Belt

The Ecliptic (Yellow): The plane of Earth's orbit and the apparent path of the Sun.

Celestial Equator (Cyan): Earth's equator projected into space, tilted at 23.5° relative to the Ecliptic.

The Zodiac Belt (Purple): Extends 9° North and South of the Ecliptic (not the equator) to safely encapsulate the slightly inclined orbits of other planets.
