Dashboard Guide

  • 360° Interaction: Touch and drag on the canvas to freely rotate the camera in full 360 degrees.
  • Zooming: Scroll your mouse wheel or use a pinch gesture on mobile devices to zoom in and out.
  • Minimize Boxes: Click the eye icon in the top header to hide all simulation text panels, giving you an unobstructed view of space.
  • Minimize Menu: Click the hamburger icon (top left) to collapse the side menu for a wider screen view.
  • Add Custom Code: Click the button at the bottom of the menu to paste raw HTML code and test new simulations in the dashboard.
