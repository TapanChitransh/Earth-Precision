Geocentric Celestial Sphere with Nakshatras

Coordinate Systems

The Ecliptic (Yellow): The apparent path of the Sun around the Earth over the course of a year.

Celestial Equator (Cyan): Earth's equator projected into space, tilted at 23.5° relative to the Ecliptic. Earth is shown at the center, aligned with this plane.

Nakshatras (Orange): The 27 Lunar Mansions of Vedic astrology. Each spans 13°20'.

Padas & Signs (Colored Ring): Each Nakshatra is split into 4 Padas (108 total). Exactly 9 Padas fit into one 30° Zodiac Sign. The outer ring is color-coded to show which Zodiac sign the Padas fall into, revealing how Nakshatras bridge across different signs.
